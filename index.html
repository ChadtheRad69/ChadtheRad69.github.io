Howdy!

I am an axe throwing coach currently studying Industrial Design at the Cleveland Institute of Art. I describe myself as controlled chaos, at least when talking about my artistic process. I like to let loose, experiment and push things further than expected, but in the end give it a professional finish that makes it look like I planned it all along. Each piece has a story and if you like what you see or want to know more check out the links below!
